Will an LLM Take My Job?

One of the big questions about how AI will effect the world is in what jobs AI will be able to replace. Naturally with all technolgoical development - people are scared about what will happen to their jobs. I want to explore this idea a bit via the tasks that AI will be able to do.

We can do this by examing the ICAREX Generative AI Impact Matrix as listed in this Medium Article (https://medium.com/@icarex/icarex-generative-ai-impact-matrix-defining-and-applying-the-strategic-tool-in-business-context-2c47ee7ab88e).
